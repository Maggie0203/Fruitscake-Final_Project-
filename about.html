ABOUT US


The premium fruit cake patisserie was created in 2012 by Henry C. An Indonesian Pastry Chef.


Commencing his passion in baking and cooking, during the time he was living in Brussels, Belgium. He came from completely different major background in Finance study environment and moved to Paris, France where he attained his official certified pastry chef in Le Cordon Bleu, Paris.

Having lived 7 years in Paris, where he pursued his dreams working in big chains of hotellerie, later he moved to Vienna, Austria to further his passion in Eastern European cake culture. Nowadays, he has his own particular market in Jakarta, where he serves his imagination and brings into reality for those who appreciates European quality fruit cake culture.

The premium line of fruit cake he offers is enhanced from different imported ingredients to maintain high level of cake quality.
